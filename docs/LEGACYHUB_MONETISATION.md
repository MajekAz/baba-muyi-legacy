# LegacyHub Monetisation Model

All pricing below is provisional and subject to market validation. Billing is deferred and must not be implemented during the MVP foundation phase.

## Provisional Pricing Note

No final prices are set in this document. Any future monthly, annual, storage, service, or enterprise price should be treated as provisional until validated with real families, archivists, institutions, and enterprise buyers.

## Staged Model

1. Demonstration and free trial.
2. Paid family plans.
3. Professional archivist and documentary service plans.
4. Institution and enterprise plans.
5. Add-on services and marketplace revenue.

## Add-Ons

- Storage upgrades.
- Future AI credits for transcription, tagging, summarisation, and draft assistance.
- Custom domains.
- Documentary production services.
- Digitisation and restoration services.
- Printed legacy books.
- White-label services.
- Professional marketplace commission.

## Revenue Principles

- Do not lock families out of exports.
- Charge for scale, storage, collaboration, privacy, support, and services.
- Keep privacy and security features available before vanity features.
- Avoid positioning LegacyHub only as a memorial product.

## Deferred Billing Work

- Stripe billing.
- Plan enforcement.
- Subscription states.
- Trial expiry automation.
- Invoice and tax handling.
- Dunning and cancellation workflows.
